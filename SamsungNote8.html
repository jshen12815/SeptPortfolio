04 - Samsung Note 8

Samsung Galaxy Note 8

Debuting the Note 8: With Apple’s well known device pages, Samsung had big competition for the new era of its site. R/GA was tapped to create a landing experience for the new Note 8 that would highlight its special features. This experience would have to be interactive, educational, and delightful. And it had to do more than display the phone - it had to overcome the stigma of Samsung’s Note 7’s exploding phones..
We worked on the family of pages for the launch of the Note 8 (codename “Great”). This included a Landing page, feature pages, compare pages, switchers pages, and additional pages targeted at specific users. Our goals were to create a dynamic experience that made the highly informational pages more engaging and relevant to the users.
